About Mod-erate Impact

    This was designed to help Twitch Streamers keep track of their Moderators contributions to their community. I stole the idea from Venalis when he had stated he was wanting something that did this exact thing. I hope that it will be useful to any streamer looking to keep track of the information provided.


Mod-erate Impact works by connecting into Twitch's IRC, and logging every message sent back and forth. From there the application will go through the written logs and count the number of times certain information is met, and perform some math to see how long between each message from a particular user was sent. Finally that information is rendered back to you, the user, as a pretty webpage. Almost everything on this page is dynamic in some way shape or form, and changes based on the messages sent within the Twitch Chat, and the settings file.


About the PINGS and the PONGS. As per RFC1459, the PING is used to test for the presence of an active client at the other end of the connection. The PONG is a reply to the PING that states "Yes I am still alive". Hence the fun little banner at the bottom about PINGING and PONGING.
